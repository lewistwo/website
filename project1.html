Kitoki Inn

Kitoki Inn offers a Japanese-inspired experience in the stunning surroundings of Bowen Island, British Columbia. The term 'kitoki' reflects themes of folklore, translating to 'tree and spirit,' fostering a renewed connection between oneself and the natural world. This retreat follows the traditional Japanese guest house style, nestled amidst woodlands and forest walks. Embracing the concept of 'bathing in nature,' Kitoki Inn provides bathhouse and spa facilities to rejuvenate and restore the mind and body.
