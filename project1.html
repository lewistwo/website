Nike Kids

You're It

For the Nike Kids' ‘You’re It’ campaign, conceptualised by Weiden + Kennedy Amsterdam, a fresh spin on the classic game of 'tag' is deployed to boost girls in sports self-confidence. Through an inspiring film, custom "Don't Skip" videos, and a YouTube game, the campaign encourages a sense of empowerment. Followers are invited to join the #youreit digital tag game on social channels in aid of contributing to a supportive sisterhood fueled by movement. The campaign was centred around go the a format-first approach. Mobile only.
