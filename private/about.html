About

A multi-disciplined graphic designer; design & typography, illustration, art direction, packaging, animation and web. Senior designer at Glasfurd and Walker. Based in London. I have 12 years experience in developing brands through identity creation, campaign stories in identity, design, and art direction, gained within both full-service design agencies and brand creative teams. Having worked in the Netherlands and Canada, Lewis is now back in London.
